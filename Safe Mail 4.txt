Subject: Semester Examination Schedule and Guidelines

Dear Students,

The Examination Branch is pleased to announce the schedule for the upcoming End Semester Examinations. The examinations will commence from July 10 and continue until July 25.

Students are advised to carefully review the timetable published on the university portal. It is the responsibility of each student to ensure that they appear for the correct examination on the specified date and time.

Important Instructions:

1. Carry your Hall Ticket and Student ID Card.
2. Arrive at the examination hall at least 30 minutes before the reporting time.
3. Mobile phones, smart watches, and electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
4. Follow all instructions provided by the invigilators.
5. Any form of malpractice will result in disciplinary action.

Students who have pending fee payments are requested to clear their dues immediately to avoid examination registration issues.

For any examination-related queries, please contact the Examination Cell during working hours.

We wish all students the very best for their examinations.

Regards,

Controller of Examinations
Institute of Aeronautical Engineering
